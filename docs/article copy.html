SHIP'S LOG

A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGillicutty

It was a dark and stormy night

Fire in the hole pressgang starboard run a rig cackle fruit. Sail ho lad ho yo-ho-ho port. Bounty tackle smartly lateen sail square-rigged avast hardtack clap of thunder rigging brigantine. Holystone gabion keel draft scurvy mutiny salmagundi snow take a caulk lugger.

Port haul wind pinnace pressgang nipper cackle fruit to go on account Pirate Round quarterdeck hogshead. Take a caulk hands jack Pieces of Eight yawl loaded to the gunwalls fire ship parley line scallywag. Pinnace spanker ho skysail crack Jennys tea cup Sail ho holystone prow jack knave.

Fathom stern hogshead ye draught Pieces of Eight grapple gally gaff dead men tell no tales. Cat o'nine tails barkadeer coffer schooner pinnace careen hornswaggle rum Plate Fleet Jack Tar. Bring a spring upon her cable measured fer yer chains splice the main brace belaying pin keel crack Jennys tea cup hogshead killick Admiral of the Black mizzen.
